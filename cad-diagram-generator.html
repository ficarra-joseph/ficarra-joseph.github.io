Project Overview

CAD Diagram Generator

This project focuses on building and characterizing a low-voltage analog audio circuit. The system uses a 555 timer oscillator to generate audio tones, a resistor-capacitor timing network to control frequency, a volume control stage, and an LM386 audio amplifier to drive a small speaker.
